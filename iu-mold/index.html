How did mold growth potential vary by room?

We looked at every room in McNutt, Teter and Foster's MoldSCORE — a number that comes from third-party testing on mold levels in residence halls.

What do the interpretations mean?

IU brought in an outside company, EMLab, to test air inside rooms in Foster, McNutt and Teter for mold. The range of the scores is from 100 to 300. These samples don't indicate the presense of mold itself, but were intended to complement existing mold tests.

  • Low — a score of less than 150 means there is a low chance of mold spores growing inside rooms.
  • Moderate — a score between 150 and 250 means there is a medium chance of mold growth inside.
  • High — a score of more than 250 means there is a high shance of mold growth inside.
